About Me

Änna Schäfers dedicates themselves to film, photography and animation under the pseudonym “Fixie Motion” and studies visual communication at the Bauhaus University in Weimar.

Their work is rooted in human storytelling, unconventional beauty and emotional atmosphere, always seeking authentic expression through the lens.
